Questions and answers

What type of organizations does The Whiteweld Foundation support?

The Whiteweld Foundation supports nonprofit organizations benefiting children in 3 areas: health; education, including arts and culture; and local philanthropy.

Do I need an invitation to submit a proposal?

Yes. If your charity serves children anywhere in the world, contact The Whiteweld Foundation with a brief description of your request and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Do you also assist needy adults, or do you focus solely on children?

Our mission is to assist young people up to the age of 18.

Who are some of the entertainers that have performed in your Concert Series?

The Whiteweld Foundation Concert Series features many exciting entertainers like Vanessa Williams, Nancy Wilson, Chuck Mangione and Frankie Valli.

May I call The Whiteweld Foundation staff to discuss proposal ideas?

We are always seeking ideas to better serve the children's organization that we fund. Please contact us here with your proposals.

Does the Foundation give grants to individuals?

Our mission is to fund only those children's organizations that best promote the health, education, and welfare of children.
