About us

Our commitment to children didn't just happen overnight. It stems from our roots as educators. Even as The Investment Firm of Whiteweld Barrister & Brown was being built into a financial and real estate empire, we never forgot our commitment to kids and made contributions for their benefit.

This concern for young people carried over to the next generation of this family-owned business, evidenced by the establishment of The Whiteweld Foundation. Its mission is to help charities fund vital educational and cultural programs, and civic events assisting children.

As a privately-held business, The Investment Banking Firm of Whiteweld Barrister & Brown created a philanthropic organization, for the purpose of helping our communities in an even bigger way.
