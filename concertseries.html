Concert series

If you love great entertainment that touches your heart and soul, and helps support the health, education and promise of some really great kids, The Whiteweld Foundation Concert Series is the ticket. A very hot ticket in fact. Over the years, our kid causes have shared the stage with some of the biggest names in Jazz, Blues, Country, New Age, and The Oldies But Goodies. The 2013 concert schedule promises more big stars and unforgettable performances. So check back soon and get ready for lots of wonderful nights out, enjoying your favorite stars, while helping kids look forward to a brighter future.
