Foundation programs

In conjunction with other global companies, The Whiteweld Foundation has sponsored important civic and local events for the benefit of children of all ages. Communities Working Together

Whether donating money to local charitable organizations or to national charities in the form of grants, the focus is always the same - children. By making a difference now in their formative years, their true potential for achievement will be enhanced dramatically. Through educational programs, engaging arts & cultural events, and community service, we seek to empower the youth of today to fulfill their dreams of tomorrow.
