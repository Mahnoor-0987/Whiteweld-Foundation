Mission

The Whiteweld Foundation's mission is to promote the health, education, and welfare of children in our communities throughout the United States and abroad.

Kids today are challenged with finding their way in a world that is more formidable than ever. By encouraging a sense of hope and self-expression in today's younger generation, we can all help to ensure a brighter social and economic future for all Americans. Civic and local community services, scholarships, and cultural arts programs are the primary means in which we are constantly seeking new charitable opportunities to uplift young people and guide them to greatness.

One example is The Whiteweld Foundation Concert Series, which enlists well-known entertainers to perform at the most notable concert halls in the country and by sharing their star power and legendary talents, raise awareness and precious funds for charitable organizations who support child health, education, and foster creativity in children of every age group.
